Explorer Overview

Use the search above, or choose one of the lookup tools below.

Archive
Suggested use: enter a block height to load its block overview, use “Block Orders” to inspect all orders in that block, use “Block Masternodes” to see which nodes were active and paid, or enter a Noso address to check its V1 balance. Numeric searches from 0 through 180031 are treated as block heights. Numeric values from 00 through 99 can also be looked up in the Governance section.
Block lookupCalls getblocksinfo and shows timestamps, hash, target, solution, minted address, reward, and fees.
Block ordersCalls getblockorders and renders each order as a readable card with sender, receiver, amount, fee, and reference.
Block masternodesCalls getblockmns and lists the masternodes active in that block. Masternodes began at block 48010; an active masternode in a block was paid even though no normal order records the payment.
Address lookupCalls getaddressbalance and preserves the current balance / incoming / outgoing logic from your existing page.
Pseudo walletPaste multiple Noso addresses to total them together as a read-only wallet view. Each address is queried individually with getaddressbalance.
Governance lookupSearches the fixed list of 100 current GVT governance slots and can also load a selected address balance.
Exchange addressesTags the three known exchange deposit/collection addresses in address lookup, block orders, and pseudo wallet results. SevenSeas, Txbit, Biconomy, Finexbox, and DogCash Wallet are also listed as historical exchange/wallet references where a shared NosoV1 address is unknown.

Historical Chain Notes

Genesis miner / first keypairN4PeJyqj8diSXnfhxSQdLpo8ddXTaGd — alias erasmus
Original masternode model10% of the block reward shared evenly; stake initially 0.15% of circulating supply and later 0.20%.
Later masternode modelStake capped at 10,500 NOSO. After PoS ended at block 88399, the masternode share began at 40%, rising 1% every 4,000 blocks to a 60% maximum.
Project fundsBeginning at block 88399, 10% of each block reward was allocated to NpryectdevepmentfundsGE.
Random jackpot addressNPrjectPrtcRandmJacptE5 received PoPW/PoP pool balances that remained inactive for 1008 blocks. Its remaining balance was used to operate project nodes.
Protocol authorityProtocol-related commands could only be performed from NosoNode wallets containing the erasmus keys.
Project-run masternodesKnown project-controlled masternode addresses are labeled throughout address, order, masternode, and pseudo-wallet results.
